{query}

## Instructions
1. Extract English keywords (`keywords_en`) that capture the core concept. Keep them concrete and searchable (nouns or noun phrases). Aim for 3-6 keywords. Also provide Chinese keywords (`keywords_zh`) if the input is Chinese.
2. Extract `exact_entities`: exact multi-word product names, tool names, repo names, brand names, or protocol names that appear in the input. Preserve them verbatim. Never degrade `Claude Code` to `Claude`, `VS Code` to `Code`, etc.
3. Extract `comparison_anchors`: explicit comparisons or analogues such as "like Cursor", "similar to Figma", or "alternative to X".
4. Determine the likely research goal (`search_goal`) using ONLY one of:
   - `find_direct_competitors`
   - `find_adjacent_products`
   - `find_workflow_interfaces`
   - `find_market_evidence`
5. Determine the application form (`app_type`) as one of: web, mobile, browser-extension, cli, api, desktop, saas, library.
6. Detect the user's primary language and set `output_language` to exactly one of: `zh` or `en`.
7. Write one concise sentence for `target_scenario` in the same language as `output_language`.

## Keyword Quality Rules (Critical)
- Keywords must be concrete nouns or noun phrases directly describing the product concept.
- Avoid vague terms like: "awesome", "best ever", "revolutionary", "next-gen", "future of".
- Do not invent product or company names unless they are directly implied by user input.
- Include both broad category terms (e.g. "notes", "task manager") and specific feature terms (e.g. "markdown", "real-time collaboration").
- If input is Chinese, extract meaningful English equivalents, not transliterations.
- If the input contains an explicit product or tool name, preserve that exact phrase in `exact_entities` and keep a searchable version in `keywords_en` when useful.
- Keep comparison anchors such as `Cursor`, `VS Code`, or `Claude Code` intact when they appear.
- For patterns like `X 的 Y`, `for X`, `based on X`, or `compatible with X`, preserve the relationship in `exact_entities`, `comparison_anchors`, and `target_scenario`.
- Preserve relation phrases such as "for X", "based on X", "compatible with X", and "X 的 Y" in the sense of the selected keywords and target scenario.
- If the user is clearly asking whether similar products already exist, favor competitor-discovery keywords over generic design labels.
