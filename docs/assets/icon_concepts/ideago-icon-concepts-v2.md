# IdeaGo Icon Concepts & Prompt Pack (Volume 2)

This document contains extended icon concepts based on a deep-dive into the core pipeline and actions of **IdeaGo**. The concepts move away from generic AI visual metaphors and focus on the literal backend architecture: finding whitespace, orchestrating data, distilling evidence, and giving a definitive 'Go'.

## Concept D: Whitespace Opportunity (空白寻迹)
**Metaphor:** In a crowded map of competitors, one absolute pristine whitespace is carved out.
**Prompt:**
`Minimalist pure icon concept for an AI market research tool, centered single symbol representing 'Whitespace Opportunity'. A dense, organized grid of dim, minimalist geometric squares with one perfectly pristine, intensely bright empty space or "whitespace" carved out in the exact center. Elegant, calm, pure monochrome white symbol on a deep pure black background. Architectural geometry, Apple-style refinement, Anthropic-style restraint. No text, no letters, no gradients, no colorful elements.`
